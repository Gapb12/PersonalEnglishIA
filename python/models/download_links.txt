# Echo English Tutor Models

Download these files and place them in the python/models/ directory:

1. LLM (Llama-3-8B-Instruct-v2-Q4_K_M.gguf):
https://huggingface.co/MaziyarPanahi/Llama-3-8B-Instruct-v2-GGUF/resolve/main/Llama-3-8B-Instruct-v2-Q4_K_M.gguf

2. TTS Voice (Amy Medium ONNX):
https://huggingface.co/rhasspy/piper-voices/resolve/main/en/en_US/amy/medium/en_US-amy-medium.onnx

3. TTS Config:
https://huggingface.co/rhasspy/piper-voices/resolve/main/en/en_US/amy/medium/en_US-amy-medium.onnx.json

Note: Whisper models will be downloaded automatically by faster-whisper on first run.
